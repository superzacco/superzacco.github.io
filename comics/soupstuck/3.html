The caparacians developed a civilization for themselves. A kingdom ruled over by none other than a black and white king. They haven't changed a bit.

The salamanders willingly became part of the working class. The funky little amphibians quite enjoyed providing a backbone for the entire planet.

The humans developed and lived off of convenient future technology. There haven't been many gigantic leaps in technological ability, only inventions to make day to day life easier for every species.

And finally, the trolls. They have been living along side humans as their co-equals. In an astonishingly un-realistic turnout, everyone voted to see if space racism was good or bad. Everyone decided that it was unanimously a terrible concept.
